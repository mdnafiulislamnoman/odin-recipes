Dim Vaji

Description

Today we are going to cook my favourite recipe called Dim Vaji. My mom used to cook it while i dont like to eat other dishes. I learn it from my mom and lets dive in to the cooking algorithm of it.

  1. Put pan to the stove.
  2. put some oil in it.
  3. In another pot stir these ingredients: egg,onion,salt,Pepper.
  4. Check if the oil is hot.
  5. Put those ingredients in that hot oil and wait for get it to set.
  6. try to make it round shaped and reverse the side.
  7. Serve it
