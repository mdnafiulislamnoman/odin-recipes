Alu vorta

Description

Today we are going to cook my another recipe called Alu vorta. There is no Bengali who didnt liked it with Dal and rice. I learned it from my mom and lets dive in to the cooking algorithm of it.

  1. Put pan to the stove.
  2. Fry some chilli pepper
  3. Take a bowl and put that fried pepper with onion,salt and mustard oil
  4. Smash it untill it mixed up
  5. Now in this bowl put boiled Potato and smash again
  6. Ready to serve
