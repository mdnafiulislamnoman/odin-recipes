Dal

Description

Today we are going to cook my another favourite recipe called Dal. There is no Bengali who didnt liked it. I I learned it from my mom and lets dive in to the cooking algorithm of it.

  1. Put pan to the stove.
  2. In another pot stir these ingredients: jira, pepper, salt, soyabin oil,lentils, tomato
  3. put some water in to them
  4. Make it cook for 15 minutes.
  5. Wait untill water get reduced
  6. Serve it
