Cranberry Orange Bread

Cranberry Orange Bread Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this absolutely irresistible cranberry orange bread recipe:

  • Flour: This easy cranberry orange bread starts with all-purpose flour, which adds structure.
  • Sugar: White sugar adds the perfect amount of sweetness.
  • Leaveners: Baking soda and baking powder act as leaveners, which means they help the bread rise.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the other flavors.
  • Butter: Softened butter adds moisture, flavor, and richness.
  • Orange: You'll need ¾ cup orange juice (fresh or from a carton) and a tablespoon of orange zest.
  • Egg: A beaten egg acts as a binder, which means it helps hold the batter together.

How to Make Cranberry Orange Bread

Making bakery-worthy cranberry orange bread is easier than you think. You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here's a brief overview of what you can expect: Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl. Stir in the butter, then add the remaining wet ingredients. Fold in the cranberries and walnuts. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake until a toothpick comes out clean.

How to Make Cranberry Orange Muffins

You can also use this recipe to make mouthwatering muffins! Just make the batter according to the recipe, then spoon it into greased muffin cups. Bake at 375 degrees F for 15 to 20 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
