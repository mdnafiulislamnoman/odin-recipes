Dim Vaji Recipe

Description

Today, I'm excited to share one of my favorite recipes, Dim Vaji. This dish has a special place in my heart because it's something my mom used to cook, and I loved it so much that I had to learn how to make it myself. Let's dive into the steps of preparing this delicious recipe!

Ingredients:
  • Eggs
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil (for frying)
Instructions:
  1. Place a pan on the stove and heat it.
  2. Add some oil to the pan.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, chopped onion, salt, and pepper.
  4. Check if the oil in the pan is hot enough.
  5. Once the oil is hot, pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  6. Let it cook until it sets, then gently shape it into a round form.
  7. Flip it over to cook the other side.
  8. Once both sides are golden brown, remove from the pan and serve.

    9. Enjoy your Dim Vaji!
